# Privacy Policy for BananaMeter

**Last updated: December 29, 2025**

## Introduction
BananaMeter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application collects, uses, and discloses information.

## 1. Camera Usage (ARKit)
Resulting from its core functionality, BananaMeter requires access to your device's camera to Measure objects using Augmented Reality (AR).
*   **How we use it:** The camera stream is processed locally on your device using Apple's ARKit framework to detect surfaces and place measurement points.
*   **Data Retention:** We do **not** record, save, store, or transmit any video or image data from your camera to any external server. The camera feed is processed in real-time and discarded immediately.

## 2. Data Collection and Ads
We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from our users.

However, we use **Google AdMob** to display banner advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use data (such as your device's Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads.
*   For more information on how Google uses detailed data, please visit [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. Third-Party Services
The app uses the following third-party services which may collect their own data:
*   **Google AdMob:** For displaying advertisements.
*   **Apple ARKit:** Information collected by Apple is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: [https://github.com/TWICE9/BananaMeter](https://github.com/TWICE9/BananaMeter)
